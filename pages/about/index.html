About me

Before, I was complaining against Java and its tooling. Now I have Clojure. I still complain, for not having discovered it earlier.

me

I'm Olivier, and I live and work near the sweet and sour city of Paris.

Java architect for over 20 years, I completely switched to Clojure some years ago for an ever increasing pleasure.

The purpose of this blog is to share some tips on this fabulous language, with the side effect1 of working my written English.

If you want to get in touch with me you can email me or find me on GitHub.
